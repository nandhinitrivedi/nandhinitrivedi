About Me

I am a dedicated Industrial Design student at PES University specializing in user-centric product development. My design philosophy is rooted in blending rigorous market research with clean, minimalist aesthetics to build sustainable hardware and lifestyle products.

My expertise spans the entire design process, from user research and persona mapping to rapid prototyping and model making. I have applied these skills professionally through freelance design and an internship with Euro Kids, delivering impactful, print-ready visual communication. Driven by a commitment to academic and creative excellence, I am a proud recipient of the CNR Scholarship and an active organizer for events like the Aatmatrisha Fest.
